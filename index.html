AI

Social Listening & NVIDIA GPU Monitoring

Automated social listening tool that monitors Reddit & Hacker News for NVIDIA GPU discussions, delivers sentiment analysis and audio briefings via email.

Python AI Analysis Automation

Inspiration

I wanted to help NVIDIA product managers stay in the loop on what people were saying about their GPUs—without spending hours scrolling through Reddit and Hacker News.

The Problem

Tracking what real people think across scattered online conversations is tedious. You miss important signals or waste time sifting through noise.

Key Features

Smart Categorization

Automatically sorts discussions into buckets like tech issues, availability, pricing, and performance.

Sentiment Analysis

Grades how people feel and flags what needs attention first.

Audio Briefings

Get an AI-narrated summary as an MP3—perfect for listening during your commute.

Automated Delivery

Set it and forget it. Reports land in your inbox on schedule or on demand.

How It Works

1. Monitor

Scrapes Reddit & Hacker News for GPU chatter

2. Analyze

AI sorts it and scores the vibe

3. Generate

Builds a clean report + audio recap

4. Deliver

Lands in your inbox with an MP3
